## 6. Improvement Opportunities

### 6.1 ⚠️ Site Structure

**Issues:**
- Single-page design limits SEO (can't target multiple keywords effectively)
- No separate service pages (harder to rank for "cotton brokerage" vs "cotton consultancy")
- No blog/news section (missing content marketing opportunity)
- No case studies or portfolio (no proof of results)
- No testimonials or client reviews (missing social proof)

**Recommendations:**
- Convert to multi-page site with dedicated service pages
- Add blog for thought leadership
- Create case studies section
- Add client testimonials

---

### 6.2 ⚠️ Contact Information

**Missing:**
- Physical address (trust + local SEO)
- Phone number (direct contact)
- Email address (alternative to form)
- Office hours
- LinkedIn company page link

**Impact:**
- Harder for clients to reach you
- Missed local SEO opportunity
- Less trustworthy (no verifiable location)

**Recommendation:**
- Add full contact details in footer
- Create dedicated contact page
- Add map integration (Google Maps)

---

### 6.3 ⚠️ Legal & Compliance

**Missing:**
- Privacy Policy page
- Terms & Conditions page
- Cookie Policy
- GDPR compliance notice

**Impact:**
- Legal risk (UK GDPR requirements)
- Reduced trust
- May violate regulations

**Recommendation:**
- Add all legal pages (can use templates)
- Implement cookie consent banner
- Update contact form with GDPR checkbox

---

### 6.4 ⚠️ Analytics & Tracking

### 6.5 ⚠️ SEO

**Issues:**

- Unknown if optimized meta tags

### 6.6 ⚠️ Conversion Optimization

**Issues:**
- Generic "I am interested!" CTA (not specific)
- Only one conversion path (contact form)
- No phone number (some prefer calling)
- No live chat option
- No calendar booking link

**Recommendation:**
- Specific CTAs per section ("Get a Quote", "Book Consultation", "Learn More")
- Add phone number + click-to-call
- Add Cal.com or Calendly booking
- Consider live chat (business hours)
